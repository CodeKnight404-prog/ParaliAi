The Problem

WHY PUNJAB
IS BURNING

Every October–November, millions of farmers set fire to crop stubble. A 40-day window, a 500-year-old habit, and a crisis that suffocates an entire subcontinent.

🔥
01 — ROOT CAUSE

The 40-Day Trap

Farmers have only 2–4 weeks between rice harvest and wheat sowing. Burning is fast and free. No machine, no time, no alternative — or so it seems.

🌫️
02 — HEALTH IMPACT

Air Quality Crisis

Burning parali releases PM2.5, CO₂, SO₂, and black carbon. AQI in Delhi and Punjab spikes beyond 500 — 20× safe levels. Millions develop respiratory illness.

🪱
03 — SOIL DAMAGE

Destroying the Land

Heat above 33°C kills soil microorganisms, fungi, and bacteria. Short-term ash nutrients cannot compensate for long-term biological destruction.

💸
04 — THE GAP

Solutions Exist — Access Doesn't

Government-subsidized Happy Seeders, Mulchers and Balers exist. But awareness, machine-sharing, logistics, and recommendations remain broken. AI can fix this.

System Modules

HOW THE
AI WORKS

🧠

Risk Prediction Engine

Random Forest classifier trained on Punjab farmer data. Takes 8 input parameters and classifies burning probability into Low, Medium, or High risk categories.

scikit-learn · Python
💡

Recommendation System

Rule-based + ML hybrid that maps farmer conditions to optimal alternatives: composting, mushroom farming, biomass selling, animal feed, or biofuel generation.

Decision Tree · Flask API
🚜

Machine Sharing Network

K-means clustering groups nearby farmers with similar needs. Optimizes Happy Seeder, Mulcher & Rotavator sharing schedules to reduce per-farmer cost.

K-Means Clustering
🌍

Pollution Calculator

Estimates CO₂ emissions, PM2.5, AQI impact, and carbon footprint for a given farm if burned. Compares against sustainable alternatives with eco-score rating.

Environmental Modeling
📊

Farmer Dashboard

Simple, accessible UI supporting Punjabi & Hindi. Icon-based navigation for low-literacy users. Designed for village officers, NGOs, and agricultural centers.

HTML · CSS · JS
🏛️

Govt. Scheme Integrator

Maps available CRM subsidies, nearby machine rental centers, and government support programs relevant to the farmer's district, residue type, and budget.
